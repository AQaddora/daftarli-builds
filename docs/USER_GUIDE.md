## Getting Started

### Installation

Choose the appropriate version for your device:

- **Android**: Download and install the APK file
- **iOS**: Install as a Progressive Web App (PWA) from Safari
- **Windows/macOS**: Download and install the desktop application
- **Web**: Access directly at [daftarli.com](https://daftarli.com)

### Creating Your Account

1. Open Daftarli
2. Click "Start Free" or "Register"
3. Enter your business information:
   - Business name
   - Your name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Password
4. Click "Create Account"

### First-Time Setup

After creating your account:

1. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add business logo (optional)
   - Set business address
   - Configure tax settings

2. **Set Up Products** (for retail businesses)
   - Add your first products
   - Set prices and costs
   - Enter stock quantities

3. **Configure Preferences**
   - Choose your currency
   - Set language (Arabic/English)
   - Configure receipt settings

## For Business Owners

### Dashboard Overview

The dashboard gives you a quick overview of your business:

- **Today's Sales**: Total revenue for today
- **Monthly Revenue**: Current month's performance
- **Pending Transactions**: Items needing attention
- **Quick Stats**: Customers, products, inventory levels

### Managing Your Business

#### Recording Sales

1. Click "New Sale" or press `Alt+N`
2. Select customer (or create new)
3. Add products:
   - Scan barcode
   - Search by name
   - Browse catalog
4. Review total amount
5. Choose payment method:
   - Cash
   - Credit/Account
   - Card
   - Multiple payment methods
6. Complete sale and print receipt

#### Tracking Expenses

1. Go to "Transactions" > "New Expense"
2. Enter expense details:
   - Category
   - Amount
   - Description
3. Attach receipt photo (optional)
4. Save expense

#### Managing Inventory

1. Navigate to "Inventory"
2. Add new products or update existing ones
3. Track stock levels
4. Set low stock alerts
5. Update prices and costs

#### Customer Management

1. Go to "Customers"
2. Add new customer accounts
3. Set credit limits
4. View customer purchase history
5. Track outstanding balances

### Financial Reports

Access comprehensive reports:

1. **Profit & Loss Statement**
   - Revenue breakdown
   - Expense categories
   - Net profit calculation

2. **Cash Flow Report**
   - Cash in/out movements
   - Period comparisons
   - Trend analysis

3. **Sales Reports**
   - Top-selling products
   - Sales by period
   - Customer analysis

4. **Inventory Reports**
   - Stock levels
   - Low stock alerts
   - Product valuation

## For Traders & Merchants

### Quick Operations

#### Fast Checkout

Use keyboard shortcuts for faster service:

- `Alt+N`: New sale
- `Alt+E`: New expense
- `Alt+C`: Calculator
- `Alt+K`: View all shortcuts
- `Alt+/`: Search

#### Barcode Scanning

1. Click the barcode icon or press scan button
2. Use device camera or barcode scanner
3. Product automatically added to cart
4. Adjust quantity if needed

#### Multiple Payment Methods

Accept various payment types:

1. **Cash**: Record cash transactions
2. **Card**: Track card payments
3. **Account/Credit**: Offer credit to trusted customers
4. **Mixed**: Combine multiple payment methods

### Inventory Management

#### Adding Products

1. Click "Add Product" in Inventory
2. Enter product details:
   - Name (Arabic and English)
   - Barcode/SKU
   - Category
   - Selling price
   - Cost price
   - Initial stock quantity
3. Save product

#### Stock Adjustments

1. Find product in inventory
2. Click "Adjust Stock"
3. Choose adjustment type:
   - Receive new stock
   - Return/damage
   - Manual correction
4. Enter quantity and reason
5. Save adjustment

### Customer Accounts

#### Creating Customer Accounts

1. Go to "Customers" > "Add Customer"
2. Enter customer details
3. Set credit limit (if applicable)
4. Save customer

#### Recording Credit Sales

1. Start new sale
2. Select customer
3. Add products
4. Choose "Account" as payment method
5. Complete transaction

#### Collecting Payments

1. Go to customer account
2. Click "Receive Payment"
3. Enter amount received
4. Choose payment method
5. Save payment

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't log in?

**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection (if using online mode)
- Verify username and password
- Use "Forgot Password" if needed
- Try offline mode if available

#### Transaction not saved?

**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Ensure sufficient device storage
- Try syncing manually
- Check transaction in drafts

#### Reports showing incorrect data?

**Solutions:**
- Verify date range selection
- Check filters applied
- Sync data if using cloud
- Contact support if issue persists

#### App running slow?

**Solutions:**
- Clear app cache
- Archive old data
- Free up device storage
- Update to latest version

#### Barcode scanner not working?

**Solutions:**
- Grant camera permissions
- Check camera is not in use by another app
- Try manual entry
- Restart the app

## Glossary
