## User Guides

### For Traders & Business Owners

#### Setting Up Your Store
1. Navigate to Settings > Business Profile
2. Enter your business name, address, and contact information
3. Upload your business logo (optional)
4. Set your default currency and tax rates
5. Save your settings

#### Managing Products
1. Go to Dashboard > Inventory
2. Click "Add Product"
3. Enter product details (name, price, cost, barcode)
4. Set initial stock quantity
5. Save the product

#### Creating Invoices
1. Go to Dashboard > Transactions
2. Click "New Sale" or press Alt+N
3. Select customer (or create new)
4. Add products by scanning barcode or searching
5. Review total and accept payment
6. Print or send digital receipt

#### Tracking Expenses
1. Go to Dashboard > Transactions
2. Click "New Expense"
3. Enter expense details and amount
4. Attach receipt photo (optional)
5. Save the expense

#### Generating Reports
1. Go to Dashboard > Reports
2. Select report type (Sales, Expenses, Profit/Loss, etc.)
3. Choose date range
4. View or export the report

### For Customers

#### Shopping Experience
1. Visit your merchant's store link
2. Browse available products
3. Add items to your cart
4. Choose payment method
5. Complete purchase and receive digital receipt

#### Viewing Your Account
1. Log in to your account
2. View transaction history
3. Check account balance
4. Download receipts

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Problem: App won't install on Android**
- Solution: Enable "Install from unknown sources" in your device settings

**Problem: Can't log in**
- Solution: Check your internet connection and verify your credentials
- Try the "Forgot Password" option if needed

**Problem: Data not syncing**
- Solution: Check your internet connection
- Go to Settings > Sync and try manual sync

**Problem: App is slow**
- Solution: Clear old data from Settings > Database
- Ensure you have enough free storage space

**Problem: Can't find a feature**
- Solution: Use the keyboard shortcut Alt+K to view all shortcuts
- Check the Help section in the app
