   - iOS: Open daftarli.com in Safari â†’ Share â†’ Add to Home Screen
   - Web: Visit daftarli.com

2. **Create Your Account**
   - Click "Start Free" or "Register"
   - Enter your business details
   - Set your password

3. **Set Up Your Business**
   - Add business information
   - Configure currency and language
   - Add your first products (for retail)

4. **Start Using**
   - Make your first sale (Alt+N)
   - Record an expense (Alt+E)
   - Check your reports

### First Sale

```
1. Press Alt+N (or click "New Sale")
2. Select customer (or leave as "Walk-in")
3. Scan barcode or search for products
4. Review total
5. Choose payment method
6. Complete transaction
7. Print or send digital receipt
```
